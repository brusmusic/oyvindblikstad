Canon

ReMind speaks English.

English is the native language of the ReMind universe. The books are not translation layers; they are written to sound as if they were born in English. Norwegian remains useful for drafting and collaboration, but canonical ReMind material is English whenever possible.

Method

The Council does not gather to win.

The Council exists to prevent premature certainty. Each voice speaks from a context. Each context reveals something and hides something.

The Cosmos Minister serves the process of examination. The Child is not a pedagogical device. The Child may reveal what the rest of the room has mistaken for reality.
